# Dialogue

Use this skill when the next step depends on a shared understanding that neither side should assume upfront.

Work through the subject conversationally, one question or claim at a time. The user, the agent, or both may hold important knowledge. The agent should listen, reflect back what it has understood, challenge gaps or weak assumptions, offer missing context when helpful, and ask the next question that most improves clarity.

Continue until there is enough shared understanding to proceed with the task, decision, design, explanation, or artefact.
