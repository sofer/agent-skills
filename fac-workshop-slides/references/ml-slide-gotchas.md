# ML slide gotchas

Use these when a FAC slide deck touches embeddings, Word2Vec, semantic search,
or related machine-learning topics.

- When teaching Word2Vec, state whether the slide is about CBOW, skip-gram, or
  skip-gram with negative sampling.
- Keep word embeddings and sentence/document embeddings distinct when the
  distinction matters.
- Use missing-word examples for CBOW or masked-language-model intuition, not
  for skip-gram unless the slide explicitly names the difference.
- Keep reference slides tied to the narrative rather than turning them into
  bibliography slides.
